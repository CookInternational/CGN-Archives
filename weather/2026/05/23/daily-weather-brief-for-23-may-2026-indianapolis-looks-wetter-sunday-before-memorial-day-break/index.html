Indianapolis Looks Wetter Sunday Before Memorial Day Break

Central Indiana’s better shower and thunderstorm chances are focused on Sunday, with Memorial Day trending warmer.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 5:16 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 5:16 PM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | The National Weather Service forecast for Indianapolis shows the more meaningful weekend rain chance focused on Sunday, with showers and thunderstorms likely before the afternoon and additional scattered storms possible later.

The forecast calls for a Sunday high near 79 and an 80 percent precipitation chance. Rainfall amounts should generally stay light, though thunderstorms can locally produce higher totals.

Additional Reporting By: National Weather Service Indianapolis; NOAA

What this means

For Central Indiana, the most weather-sensitive window is Sunday, especially for outdoor events, travel timing and race-weekend plans.
