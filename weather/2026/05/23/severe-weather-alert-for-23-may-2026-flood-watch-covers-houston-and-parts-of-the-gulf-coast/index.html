Severe Weather Alert for 23 May 2026: Flood Watch Covers Houston and Parts of the Gulf Coast

Official National Weather Service products show flood-watch conditions through Memorial Day for parts of Southeast Texas and Louisiana.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published 23 May 2026 at 5:14 PM EDT

HOUSTON | The National Weather Service shows a Flood Watch for Houston from Saturday into Monday evening, with thunderstorms likely and additional rainfall possible through the holiday weekend.

NWS Lake Charles also reported a Flood Watch for southeast Texas and southwest, south and central Louisiana until 7 p.m. Memorial Day, with several rounds of showers and thunderstorms expected.

Additional Reporting By: National Weather Service Houston; National Weather Service Lake Charles

What this means

The flood-risk story is focused on timing and placement. The most important updates are official warnings, rainfall totals, road conditions and whether Monday’s watch area changes.
