Memorial Day Weather Splits Between Midwest Showers and Gulf Coast Flood Risk

Indianapolis has a Sunday thunderstorm chance while the Gulf Coast carries more formal flood-watch concern.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 4:46 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 4:46 PM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | The Memorial Day weekend weather map is split between manageable Midwest shower chances and more serious Gulf Coast flood-watch conditions.

The National Weather Service forecast for Indianapolis shows showers and thunderstorms likely Sunday. Houston and parts of southeast Texas and Louisiana remain under flood-watch products into Memorial Day.

Additional Reporting By: National Weather Service Indianapolis; National Weather Service Houston; National Weather Service Lake Charles

What this means

The practical distinction is between a forecast and an alert. Indianapolis has rain and thunderstorm chances; parts of the Gulf Coast have official flood-watch products.
