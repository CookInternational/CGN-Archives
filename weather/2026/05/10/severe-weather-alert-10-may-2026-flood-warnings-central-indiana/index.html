Severe Weather Alert for 10 May 2026: Flood Warnings Issued Across Central Indiana

Residents advised to prepare for potential flooding as heavy rainfall is expected.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published At: 10 May 2026 at 5:59 AM EDT · Last Updated At: 10 May 2026 at 9:07 AM EDT

The National Weather Service has issued flood warnings for multiple counties in central Indiana as heavy rainfall is anticipated throughout the day. Residents in affected areas are advised to monitor local conditions and take necessary precautions to ensure their safety.

Forecasts indicate that rainfall amounts could reach up to 3 inches in some locations, leading to potential flash flooding in low-lying areas and near rivers. The NWS recommends that residents avoid driving through flooded roads and to stay indoors during severe weather conditions.

As the storm system moves through the region, it is crucial for residents to stay updated on the latest weather alerts and to have an emergency plan in place. Those living in flood-prone areas should prepare for possible evacuations and ensure that emergency kits are readily available.

Local authorities are monitoring the situation closely and will provide updates as necessary. Community members are encouraged to report any flooding incidents to local emergency services.

What this means

Residents in central Indiana should take the flood warnings seriously and prepare for possible flooding. This includes avoiding travel in affected areas, securing property, and having an emergency plan ready. Staying informed through local news and weather updates will be crucial in ensuring safety during this weather event.
