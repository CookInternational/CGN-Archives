Daily Weather Brief for 8 May 2026: Mild Temperatures with Afternoon Rain Showers Expected

A look at the weather conditions for Indianapolis and central Indiana today.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published 8 May 2026 at 3:06 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | Today, central Indiana is expected to see mild temperatures with a high reaching approximately 70°F. The day will start off relatively clear, but clouds will increase throughout the morning, leading to rain showers by the afternoon.

As the day progresses, residents should prepare for scattered showers, particularly during the afternoon hours. Rainfall amounts are expected to be light, but the timing may affect outdoor plans and the evening commute.

Winds will be gentle, blowing from the southeast at about 10 to 15 mph, which may add a slight chill to the air, especially during the rain. Humidity levels will be moderate, contributing to a comfortable feel for most of the day.

For those with outdoor activities planned, it would be wise to have an umbrella or raincoat handy. The rain is expected to taper off by the evening, allowing for a clearer night sky.

Looking ahead, the weekend forecast indicates a return to warmer temperatures with more sunshine, making it ideal for outdoor events and activities.
