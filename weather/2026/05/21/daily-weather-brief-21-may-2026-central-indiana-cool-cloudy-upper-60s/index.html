Daily Weather Brief for 21 May 2026: Cool, Cloudy Day Keeps Central Indiana in the Upper 60s

Central Indiana should stay cooler today, with clouds, early showers and temperatures topping out near 67.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published At: 21 May 2026 at 8:35 AM EDT · Last Updated At: 21 May 2026 at 8:35 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | Central Indiana should stay cooler today, with clouds, early showers in some areas and afternoon temperatures holding near the upper 60s.

The corrected forecast keeps Indianapolis and nearby communities well below summerlike levels. A high near 67 means the day should feel cool for late May, especially during the morning commute and early school hours when clouds and damp spots may linger.

Rain chances are most useful for planning early in the day. Drivers should allow a little extra time where pavement is wet, and families heading to school drop-offs, appointments or downtown errands may want a light jacket or umbrella before conditions become more manageable later.

The afternoon and evening should remain more comfortable than hot. Event crews, outdoor workers and families planning race-week setup around Central Indiana should still check the latest forecast, but the main issue is cooler clouds rather than heat.

No severe-weather language is being used for this brief without official alert support. Residents should continue checking National Weather Service updates if plans depend on timing for showers, outdoor work or evening travel.

What this means

For Central Indiana residents, today looks more like a light-jacket or umbrella-check day than a summer heat day. Outdoor plans should account for clouds, damp pavement in spots and cooler temperatures through the afternoon and evening.
