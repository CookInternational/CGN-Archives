Daily Weather Brief for 16 May 2026: Warm Temperatures and Possible Thunderstorms in Central Indiana

Central Indiana is expected to experience warm temperatures today, with a chance of thunderstorms later in the evening.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published At: 16 May 2026 at 6:07 AM EDT · Last Updated At: 16 May 2026 at 6:07 AM EDT

CITY | Central Indiana is gearing up for a warm day today, with temperatures expected to reach the mid-80s Fahrenheit. The National Weather Service indicates that while the day will start off warm and humid, a cold front moving into the area may bring thunderstorms later in the evening.

As the day progresses, residents should prepare for the possibility of scattered thunderstorms, particularly after 4 PM. The storms could bring heavy rainfall and gusty winds, so those with outdoor plans should stay alert to changing weather conditions.

Looking ahead, the warm temperatures are expected to continue into the weekend, but the chance of thunderstorms may persist as the cold front settles in. This could lead to a mix of sun and clouds, with occasional rain showers.

It is advisable for residents to stay updated on the latest forecasts and be prepared for any severe weather alerts that may be issued as the day progresses.
