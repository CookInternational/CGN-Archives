Daily Weather Brief for 29 May 2026: Dry, Comfortable Weather Opens the Weekend in Central Indiana

Central Indiana can expect dry, comfortable weather Friday with low humidity and a pleasant weekend ahead.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published At: 29 May 2026 at 6:49 AM EDT · Last Updated At: 29 May 2026 at 6:49 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | Central Indiana is heading into Friday with a quiet, comfortable weather setup and a pleasant start to the weekend.

The Indianapolis forecast calls for sunny to partly cloudy conditions with low humidity and a high near 82°F. Friday night should stay comfortable, with a low near 57°F. Saturday is expected to remain pleasant, with intervals of sunshine and a high near 78°F, followed by another mild day Sunday with high thin clouds and a high near 77°F.

The National Weather Service Indianapolis Weather Story also points toward a dry and comfortable weekend, with increasing high clouds later Friday and Saturday but no severe-weather setup for Indianapolis in the general forecast.

That means the main weather message is simple: outdoor plans are in good shape. Drivers, families, workers and event organizers should still monitor local forecasts, but the day does not require stormy framing.

CGN News is correcting earlier draft language that suggested a warmer overnight low and possible isolated showers. Current forecast support is cleaner: dry, comfortable, low humidity and a weekend that looks favorable for outdoor plans.

Additional Reporting By: National Weather Service Indianapolis; National Weather Service Indianapolis; NOAA; AccuWeather; Open-Meteo

What this means

The useful takeaway is that central Indiana has a good weather window. Expect low humidity, highs near the low 80s Friday and upper 70s this weekend, with no severe-weather framing needed for the general Indianapolis forecast.
