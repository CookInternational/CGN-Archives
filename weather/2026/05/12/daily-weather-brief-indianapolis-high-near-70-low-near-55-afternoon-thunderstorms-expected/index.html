As the storms approach, it is advisable to stay updated with local weather alerts and prepare for potential disruptions. Outdoor events may need to be postponed or moved indoors due to the expected weather conditions.

Looking ahead, the weekend forecast suggests a return to more stable weather, with temperatures remaining in the comfortable range. However, residents should remain vigilant and prepared for any sudden weather changes.
