Daily Weather Brief for 28 May 2026: Morning Fog Gives Way to Warm, Quiet Weather

Indianapolis starts with fog before a warm afternoon and a more comfortable Friday-Saturday stretch.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published 28 May 2026 at 8:12 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | Indianapolis starts Thursday with fog, then turns warmer as sunshine filters through high clouds and afternoon temperatures climb into the low 80s.

The current forecast calls for dense fog this morning, followed by a warm day with a high near 83°F. Friday looks partly sunny and pleasant, with Saturday bringing intervals of clouds and sun and highs in the upper 70s.

The important change from earlier drafts is that this is not an afternoon thunderstorm brief for Indianapolis. The reader-service forecast today is fog early, warmer weather later and a more comfortable stretch into Friday and Saturday.

Outdoor plans look better after the morning fog lifts, but commuters should allow extra time early and watch visibility in low-lying areas.

Additional Reporting By: National Weather Service; NOAA; AccuWeather; Open-Meteo

What this means

For readers, the best plan is to treat the early commute cautiously because of fog, then expect a warm afternoon and a calmer weather pattern heading into the end of the week.
