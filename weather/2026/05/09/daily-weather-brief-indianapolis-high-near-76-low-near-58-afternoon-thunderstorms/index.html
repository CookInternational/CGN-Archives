Daily Weather Brief for 9 May 2026: High Near 76, Low Near 58 with Afternoon Thunderstorms

Expect warm temperatures and scattered thunderstorms later in the day across Indianapolis and central Indiana.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published At: 9 May 2026 at 7:33 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 9 May 2026 at 7:33 PM EDT

Warm weather is on the horizon for Indianapolis and central Indiana on 9 May 2026, with temperatures reaching a high of 76°F and a low of 58°F. The day will start off mild, but residents should be prepared for scattered thunderstorms developing in the afternoon.

The National Weather Service indicates that the storms may bring brief heavy rainfall and gusty winds. As the day progresses, the likelihood of precipitation increases, particularly after 2 PM. Commuters should be aware of potential delays due to wet roads and reduced visibility.

Humidity levels will be moderate, making it feel a bit warmer than the actual temperature. Outdoor activities should be planned with caution, especially later in the day when thunderstorms are more likely.

For those with evening plans, it is advisable to keep an eye on the weather updates and be prepared for possible changes in conditions. The storms are expected to clear by late evening, leading to a cooler night.
