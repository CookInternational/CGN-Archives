Daily Weather Brief for 19 May 2026: Warm Temperatures and Possible Thunderstorms in Central Indiana

Central Indiana is expected to experience warm weather with a chance of thunderstorms later in the day.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published 19 May 2026 at 6:03 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | Central Indiana is set to experience warm temperatures today, reaching highs in the mid-80s Fahrenheit. The National Weather Service indicates that while the morning will be mostly sunny, conditions are expected to change as the day progresses.

As we move into the afternoon, the forecast calls for increasing cloud cover and a chance of thunderstorms. Some storms may become severe, bringing heavy rainfall and gusty winds. Residents are advised to stay updated on weather alerts as the day unfolds.

In addition to the potential for thunderstorms, humidity levels will rise, making it feel warmer than the actual temperature. This combination of heat and humidity could lead to discomfort for some, particularly those engaging in outdoor activities.

As evening approaches, the likelihood of thunderstorms increases, with the possibility of localized flooding in areas that receive heavy rainfall. It is recommended to have a plan in place for any outdoor events scheduled for later today.

What This Means: Residents of Central Indiana should prepare for a warm day with the potential for severe weather. Staying informed through local weather updates and alerts will be crucial, especially for those with outdoor plans.
