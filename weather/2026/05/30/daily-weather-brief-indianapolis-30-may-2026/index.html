Daily Weather Brief: Indianapolis Starts Cloudy Before a Warmer Weekend

Saturday should turn partly sunny with highs near the lower 80s before a pleasant Sunday with high clouds.

By Jessica Storm · Weather · Published 30 May 2026 at 9:45 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | Indianapolis starts Saturday with mostly cloudy conditions before a warmer weekend pattern settles into central Indiana.

Current conditions in Indianapolis were mostly cloudy and near 56 degrees Saturday morning. The forecast calls for intervals of clouds and sun Saturday, with a high near 81 and an overnight low near 53. Sunday is expected to stay pleasant, with high thin cloudiness, a high near 78 and a low near 58.

No CGN Severe Weather Alert is being issued with this brief. Severe Weather Alerts should only be published when supported by official alert information. Readers should still check updated National Weather Service and NOAA information before outdoor events, travel, youth sports, road work or race-weekend activities.

For families and event planners, the main practical message is simple: expect a warmer Saturday afternoon after the cloudy start, then a mild Sunday with enough cloud cover to keep the day comfortable. Keep water nearby for outdoor activity, use sun protection during brighter breaks and monitor any official forecast changes if you are traveling around central Indiana.
