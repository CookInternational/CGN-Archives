CGN Business Journal: Crisis Financing Requests Show How War Costs Spread Beyond the Battlefield

Reuters reported that 27 countries have moved to secure access to World Bank crisis funds since the Iran war began.

By CGN News Staff · Business · Published 23 May 2026 at 5:22 PM EDT

SAN FRANCISCO | Reuters reported that 27 countries have moved to ensure access to World Bank crisis-financing tools since the Iran war began.

The reported requests do not all mean funds have been drawn, but they show governments preparing for sharper pressure from energy costs, fertilizer disruption and weaker fiscal positions.

Additional Reporting By: Reuters; Reuters Energy

What this means

The business story is no longer limited to oil producers or military states. It is reaching governments, lenders, households and companies that rely on predictable shipping and energy prices.
