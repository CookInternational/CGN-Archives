CGN World Brief: Iran Talks, Ukraine’s EU Push and China Mine Disaster Shape Late-Day Agenda

Global coverage centers on Middle East negotiations, Ukraine’s accession push, China’s coal mine disaster and Serbia’s widening protests.

By CGN News Staff · World · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 5:28 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 5:28 PM EDT

LONDON | U.S.-Iran diplomacy, Ukraine’s European future, China’s mining disaster and Serbia’s street protests formed the core of the late-day global news agenda.

Reuters reported progress in talks aimed at ending the U.S.-Iran conflict, while Al Jazeera and Reuters reported that Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy told European Union leaders the time is right to begin accession talks. AP reported that a gas explosion at the Liushenyu coal mine in Shanxi killed at least 82 people.
