CGN Market Report: Warsh Takes Fed Helm as Oil, Inflation and Rate Doubts Pressure Investors

Markets are watching the new Fed chair, consumer sentiment, oil risk and whether Iran diplomacy changes the inflation path.

By CGN News Staff · Markets · Published 23 May 2026 at 5:24 PM EDT

NEW YORK | Markets entered the evening watching how Kevin Warsh’s Federal Reserve will handle inflation, whether oil risk eases and whether consumers keep spending despite weak sentiment.

Reuters reported that Warsh takes over with inflation climbing and consumer sentiment weakening. Reuters also reported that oil markets could approach a red zone later this summer if supply stress persists.

Additional Reporting By: Reuters Warsh; Reuters Consumer Sentiment; Reuters Energy

What this means

Markets are reacting to a chain of risks: fuel costs, inflation expectations, Fed credibility and the timing of a potential Iran settlement.
