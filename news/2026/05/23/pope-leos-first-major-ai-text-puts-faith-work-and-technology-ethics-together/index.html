Pope Leo’s First Major AI Text Puts Faith, Work and Technology Ethics Together

The Vatican document is expected to address artificial intelligence, workers and the use of technology in war.

By Sophie Keller · Religion & Spirituality · Published 23 May 2026 at 4:42 PM EDT

VATICAN CITY | Reuters reported that Pope Leo is set to release his first major encyclical, Magnifica Humanitas, on May 25, addressing artificial intelligence and its social consequences.

The document is expected to criticize the use of AI in warfare and examine the technology’s effect on workers’ rights.

Additional Reporting By: Reuters

What this means

The religious significance is in the framing: AI is not only a business or engineering issue, but a human dignity and social-order question.
