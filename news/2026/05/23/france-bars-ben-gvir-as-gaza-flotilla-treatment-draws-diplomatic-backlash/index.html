France Bars Ben-Gvir as Gaza Flotilla Treatment Draws Diplomatic Backlash

France banned Israel’s far-right national security minister after footage and activist allegations triggered international condemnation.

By Helena Price · World · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 5:06 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 5:06 PM EDT

PARIS | France banned Israeli National Security Minister Itamar Ben-Gvir from entering its territory after footage of his treatment of detained Gaza flotilla activists drew international condemnation.

Al Jazeera reported that the decision followed footage and allegations tied to the treatment of Gaza flotilla activists, while Reuters reported that France urged the European Union to consider similar sanctions.

Additional Reporting By: Al Jazeera; Reuters

What this means

The next diplomatic signal is whether other European states join France, whether the EU considers sanctions and whether Israel releases additional findings or legal responses.
