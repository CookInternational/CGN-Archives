Zelenskyy Presses EU Leaders to Start Ukraine Accession Process

Ukraine’s president rejected associate membership as insufficient while battlefield pressure continues.

By Amara Okafor · World · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 5:08 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 5:08 PM EDT

LONDON | Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy told European Union leaders that the time is right to begin Ukraine’s accession process, Al Jazeera reported, citing Reuters.

Zelenskyy argued that associate membership would leave Ukraine without voting rights and less ability to defend its interests inside Europe’s political structure.

Additional Reporting By: Al Jazeera

What this means

The accession story now depends on procedure and politics: whether EU leaders open the process, what conditions they attach and how quickly Ukraine can meet institutional requirements.
