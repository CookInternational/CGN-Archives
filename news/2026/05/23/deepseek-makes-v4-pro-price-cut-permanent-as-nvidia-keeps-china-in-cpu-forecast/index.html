DeepSeek Makes V4-Pro Price Cut Permanent as Nvidia Keeps China in CPU Forecast

AI competition is turning on model costs, chip access and China’s domestic compute supply.

By Daniel Cho · Technology · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 4:58 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 4:58 PM EDT

PALO ALTO | Reuters reported that DeepSeek will make a 75 percent price cut on its flagship V4-Pro model permanent.

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang separately said Nvidia’s forecast for a $200 billion CPU market includes China, even as export controls and approval issues shape the company’s access.

Additional Reporting By: Reuters DeepSeek; Reuters Nvidia

What this means

The industry signal is pricing pressure. Cheaper model access can widen adoption, but the winners still depend on compute supply and regulatory permission.
