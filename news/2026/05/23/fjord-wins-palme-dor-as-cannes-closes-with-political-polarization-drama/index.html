Fjord Wins Palme d’Or as Cannes Closes With Political Polarization Drama

Cristian Mungiu’s Norway-set film took the festival’s top prize, according to AP.

By Rick Ellis · Entertainment · Published 23 May 2026 at 4:56 PM EDT

CANNES | AP reported that Cristian Mungiu’s Norway-set drama Fjord won the Palme d’Or at the 79th Cannes Film Festival.

The win gives Mungiu his second Palme d’Or and places the film in the year’s international awards conversation.

Additional Reporting By: Associated Press

What this means

The Cannes result gives distributors and awards-watchers a clear film to track through the rest of the year.
