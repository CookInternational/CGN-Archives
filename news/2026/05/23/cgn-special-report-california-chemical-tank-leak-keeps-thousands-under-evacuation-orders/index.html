CGN Special Report: California Chemical Tank Leak Keeps Thousands Under Evacuation Orders

Orange County officials continued emergency cooling operations after a hazardous tank leak forced a large evacuation zone.

By CGN News Staff · Special Reports · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 5:30 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 5:30 PM EDT

GARDEN GROVE, Calif. | Emergency crews in Orange County continued working around a leaking chemical storage tank after authorities ordered large-scale evacuations near a Garden Grove aerospace plastics facility.

AP and BBC reporting described the public-safety concern around the damaged tank and the evacuation zone. Crews remained focused on cooling the tank, limiting exposure risk and preventing a wider incident.

Additional Reporting By: Associated Press; BBC News

What this means

The next public record to watch is whether the tank is stabilized, when evacuation orders narrow and whether environmental monitoring finds off-site impacts.
