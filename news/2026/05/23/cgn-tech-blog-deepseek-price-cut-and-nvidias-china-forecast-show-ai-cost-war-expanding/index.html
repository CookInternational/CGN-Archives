CGN Tech Blog: DeepSeek Price Cut and Nvidia’s China Forecast Show AI Cost War Expanding

China’s AI price competition and Nvidia’s China-linked CPU forecast point to a wider fight over compute access.

By Daniel Cho · Technology · Published At: 23 May 2026 at 5:20 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 23 May 2026 at 5:20 PM EDT

PALO ALTO | Reuters reported that DeepSeek will make a 75 percent price cut on its flagship V4-Pro model permanent, sharpening the price war among AI model providers.

Reuters separately reported that Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang said the company’s forecast for a $200 billion CPU market includes China, even as export controls and regulatory approvals shape what the company can sell there.

Additional Reporting By: Reuters DeepSeek; Reuters Nvidia

What this means

The industry signal is pricing pressure. Cheaper model access can widen adoption, but the winners still depend on compute supply, developer trust and regulatory permission.
