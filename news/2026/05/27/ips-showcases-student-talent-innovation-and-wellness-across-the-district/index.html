IPS Showcases Student Talent, Innovation and Wellness Across the District

Indianapolis Public Schools highlighted student arts, innovation and wellness work at a district showcase.

By Rick Ellis · Local · Published 27 May 2026 at 7:52 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | Indianapolis Public Schools is highlighting student creativity, innovation and wellness after a district showcase brought together work from students across grade levels.

IPS said its Imagine Create Perform K-12 District Showcase of the Arts, Innovation and Wellness took place at the Northwest Middle School gymnasium, where students displayed projects, performances and achievements from across the district.

The event fits a larger public-education story in Indianapolis: schools are trying to show families that student success is not limited to test scores. Arts, wellness, career exploration, innovation and student voice are all part of how districts build community confidence.

That kind of showcase also gives parents, teachers and neighbors a chance to see what students are making and learning inside classrooms they may not otherwise visit.

Additional Reporting By: Indianapolis Public Schools; Indianapolis Public Schools

What this means

For families, the story is about visibility: students get a public stage, and the district gets to show a broader picture of learning.

For the city, it is a reminder that education coverage should include student work, not only budgets, board meetings and disputes.
