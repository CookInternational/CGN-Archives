Netflix Launches Global Tour for K-Pop Demon Hunters

The tour aims to bring the popular K-Pop series to live audiences worldwide.

By Rick Ellis · Entertainment · Published 16 May 2026 at 6:19 PM EDT

Netflix has officially announced the launch of a global tour for its popular K-Pop series centered around demon hunters. This initiative is designed to connect fans with the series in a live setting, offering performances that blend music, dance, and storytelling.

The tour will kick off in major cities around the world, providing fans with an immersive experience that showcases the vibrant culture of K-Pop. Attendees can expect high-energy performances featuring the series' music, along with appearances from cast members and special guests.

This move comes as part of Netflix's broader strategy to expand its reach within the entertainment sector, particularly in the realm of live events. By bringing its content to life, the streaming giant aims to deepen viewer engagement and attract new audiences.

As K-Pop continues to gain international popularity, this tour represents a significant opportunity for Netflix to capitalize on the genre's fanbase. The company has not yet released specific dates or locations for the tour, but fans are encouraged to stay tuned for updates.

What This Means: The global tour signifies a shift in how streaming platforms are engaging with audiences, moving beyond traditional viewing experiences to create live, interactive events. This could set a precedent for future adaptations of popular series and films into live formats, enhancing fan interaction and loyalty.
