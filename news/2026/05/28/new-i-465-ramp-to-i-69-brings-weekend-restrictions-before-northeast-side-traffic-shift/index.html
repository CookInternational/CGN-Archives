New I-465 Ramp to I-69 Brings Weekend Restrictions Before Northeast-Side Traffic Shift

A new eastbound I-465 ramp to northbound I-69 is expected to open this weekend, but drivers should watch for restrictions first.

By Natalie Ward · Local · Published 28 May 2026 at 8:09 AM EDT

INDIANAPOLIS | A new ramp from eastbound I-465 to northbound I-69 is expected to open this weekend on Indianapolis’ northeast side, but drivers should watch for overnight restrictions before traffic shifts into the new pattern.

FOX59 and WTHR reported that restrictions will be in place as crews prepare to open the ramp. Clear Path 465 also lists ongoing short-term closures and long-term restrictions around the I-465/I-69 interchange.

For commuters, the improvement is welcome but the transition can be confusing. Drivers should expect changing lanes, closed ramps, reduced speeds and crews working near traffic until the pattern is fully established.

The opening is part of the larger Clear Path 465 project, which is reworking one of the region’s busiest interchange areas. That means the weekend milestone is not the end of construction, but it is a visible step toward a less congested northeast-side commute.

Additional Reporting By: FOX59; WTHR; Clear Path 465

What this means

For readers, the practical takeaway is to check the latest Clear Path 465 updates before driving the northeast side this weekend and to expect temporary confusion as traffic moves into the new pattern.
