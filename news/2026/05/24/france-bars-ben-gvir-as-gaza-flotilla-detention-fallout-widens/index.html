France Bars Ben-Gvir as Gaza Flotilla Detention Fallout Widens

France’s move followed international criticism of video showing the Israeli minister taunting detained flotilla activists.

By Helena Price · World · Published At: 24 May 2026 at 6:46 PM EDT · Last Updated At: 24 May 2026 at 6:46 PM EDT

PARIS | France barred Israeli National Security Minister Itamar Ben-Gvir from its territory after footage of him taunting detained Gaza flotilla activists triggered international condemnation.

Al Jazeera reported, citing AFP and Reuters, that France imposed the ban after the video circulated. Reuters separately reported that foreign ministers from eight Arab-Islamic countries condemned Ben-Gvir’s conduct toward flotilla participants detained by Israel.

The dispute connects European diplomacy, Gaza humanitarian politics, Israel’s internal coalition tensions and the treatment of detained activists.

The public record remains centered on the video, official condemnations, Israeli responses and any diplomatic steps by France or other governments.
