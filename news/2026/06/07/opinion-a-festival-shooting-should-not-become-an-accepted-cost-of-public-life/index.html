Opinion: A Festival Shooting Should Not Become an Accepted Cost of Public Life

Twelve people were wounded near Toledo’s Old West End Festival; public safety requires evidence-based prevention, prepared event operations and accountable gun policy.

By Natalie Ward · Opinion · Published At: 7 June 2026 at 8:19 AM EDT · Last Updated At: 7 June 2026 at 8:19 AM EDT
